WELCOME TO Chhatrapati Shahu Institute of Business Education and Research,Kolhapur

The Chhatrapati Shahu Institute of Business Education and Research was established in 1976 by Dr. A. D. Shinde. It is an autonomous Institute by the Shivaji University, Kolhapur, with the concurrence of the Government of Maharashtra and the University Grants Commission, New Delhi and the AICTE.The Chhatrapati Shahu Institute of Business Education and Research has well stocked library, separate hostels for men and women in the sprawling campus. The Institutes offers MBA, MCA, MSW courses.

The MCA course conducted by the Department is recognised by AICTE. The course emphasizes both theory and practical. Practical consist of usual practice exercises, exercises that bring of the finer points of languages and mini projects; the theoretical topics are a proper mix of topics in Computer Science, Management and Mathematics. The topics thought include the latest trends such as Data warehousing and Data mining. UML, Internet Programming and Applications such as designing e-Commerce applications, VB and VC++, Oracle, JAVA, .Net and various scripting languages.
